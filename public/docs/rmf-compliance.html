🤖 Autonomous Management & Human Oversight
All automated management actions by Spectra's AI agents are subject to a confidence-scoring gate (RecommendationScorer). High-impact changes (bidding strategy, targeting) require ≥0.85 confidence and are queued for advertiser review before applying. Lower-risk changes (negative keywords, keyword status) auto-apply at ≥0.60 confidence. Every automated action is logged with a full audit trail accessible to the advertiser in the Activity Log.
